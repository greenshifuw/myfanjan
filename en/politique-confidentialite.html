Privacy Policy

The company SAS NEOPHYTA (MyFanjan® brand) is committed to ensuring that the collection and processing of your data, carried out from the MyFanjan® site, comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act.

1. Collection of Personal Data

We collect the following data via the contact form present on our site:

  • Name and First Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Type (Local Authority, Farmer, Individual, etc.)
  • Free Message

2. Purpose of Collected Data

The information collected is subject to computer processing intended exclusively for SAS NEOPHYTA for the following purposes:

  • Respond to requests for information and quotes sent via the contact form.
  • Management of the client/prospect relationship.
  • Sending commercial information if you have explicitly consented.

3. Conservation Duration

Your personal data is kept only for the time necessary to achieve the objective pursued during their collection (for example, the time to process your quote request), then archived according to legal limitation periods.

4. Data Security

SAS NEOPHYTA implements all necessary technical and organizational measures to guarantee the security and confidentiality of the personal data collected and processed, and in particular to prevent them from being distorted, damaged, or communicated to unauthorized third parties.

5. Your Rights (Data Protection)

In accordance with applicable regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access, rectification, and erasure of data.
  • Right to limitation of processing.
  • Right to object to processing.

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at the following address:
Email: contact@myfanjan.com
Postal Address: SAS NEOPHYTA, 22 impasse Commandant Richard, 97430 Le TAMPON, Reunion Island.

6. Cookies

Browsing on the MyFanjan® site is likely to cause the installation of cookie(s) on the user's computer. A cookie is a small file, which does not allow the identification of the user, but which records information relating to the navigation of a computer on a site. The data thus obtained aims to facilitate subsequent navigation on the site, and is also intended to allow various measures of attendance.
