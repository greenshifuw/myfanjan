About Us

Who Are We?

Born in Reunion Island, MyFanjan is an initiative led by environmental engineers and entrepreneurs passionate about ecological transition and agriculture.

Our Mission

Rethinking food production to be more local, sustainable, and intelligent.

We develop vertical, sustainable, and intelligent food production to strengthen Reunion's food autonomy alongside farmers.

Cultivating Differently

Thanks to vertical hydroponics, we offer innovative production systems allowing soil-less cultivation on small surfaces, with reduced water consumption and high yield. Our gardens are designed to fit anywhere: balconies, schools, companies, urban farms, public spaces...

"For a greener, more resilient, and more inclusive territory."

Local and Eco-Innovative Know-How

Our hydroponic towers are designed and manufactured in Reunion Island, using sustainable and food-grade materials.

Each installation is custom-designed according to needs: market gardening, ornamental, or educational production. We support our clients from design to maintenance, including training on system usage.

A Circular and Inclusive Vision

At MyFanjan, we believe in agriculture that respects ecosystems and people.

Our solutions fit into a circular economy logic, valuing local resources and encouraging food autonomy. Beyond technology, we carry a vision: that of a greener, more resilient, and more inclusive territory.
