Our History

2020

The Genesis

In 2020, at the beginning of the lockdown, Franck Ferdinand, founder of MyFanjan, designed and tested prototypes to allow growing vegetables and fruits using the minimum available resources.

Frugal Innovation

Water, natural fertilizer, sun, and thanks to hydroponics no need for soil, or having a lot of space to grow salads, tomatoes, eggplants, leafy greens, and other green onions...

Thus was born the first MyFanjan tower, a hydroponic tower designed to give Reunion Islanders a simple way to cultivate on their terrace in an apartment or at home.

5 Years of R&D

Innovation & Performance

After 5 years of research and development, MyFanjan towers have reached a high level of technological maturity, offering yields optimized for our climate.

MyFanjan products are created and manufactured in Reunion Island, with a team that continuously develops solutions to expand the family of vegetables, flowers, fruits that can be grown in a small space.

100% Local & Circular

Our towers are produced locally from materials resulting from the recycling of Reunionese plastics, transformed by a partner company.

Each MyFanjan tower is therefore 100% local, sustainable, and circular: a product born here, to feed here.

MyFanjan embodies the new era of food sovereignty.
