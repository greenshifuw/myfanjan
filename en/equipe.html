The MyFanjan Team

A multidisciplinary team united by a passion for plants and innovation.

View Profile

Franck FERDINAND

Founder & Scientific Director

Trained as an ecologist scientist and environmental engineer, Franck Ferdinand has over 20 years of experience in innovative systems applied to sustainable development and ecological transition. Founder of MyFanjan, he draws on dual expertise as an international consultant (Smart Cities, Urban Agriculture) and lecturer at the University of La Réunion. He designs innovative solutions, such as locally manufactured hydroponic towers, to address food autonomy challenges.

View Profile

Liouma TOKITSU

General Manager

Entrepreneur and engineer, Liouma Tokitsu brings to MyFanjan solid experience in creating and growing technology companies. He has founded and developed innovative companies achieving several million euros in revenue, with top-tier international clients. At MyFanjan, he structures the company, steers development strategy, partnerships, and financing, and prepares for industrial and international scaling.

Laurent MATTLÉ

Technical & Innovation Expert

Electronics and computer engineer, Laurent Mattlé is a specialist in connected systems, IoT, and digital manufacturing. He designs and industrializes all of MyFanjan's technological building blocks: sensors, control units, automation, and software platforms. He ensures the reliability, scalability, and industrial capacity of MyFanjan solutions, from prototype to large-scale deployment.
